Founding Members

The MIRC was founded as McMaster's first student research team by Lucas Krenn and Nik Počuča in 2016.

Nik Počuča

CO-Founder

Nik Počuča, was a fourth-year Math and Stats student with a passion for computational statistics and machine learning algorithms. His primary interests revolved around using neural nets to classify and recognize indicators in large datasets to better improve time series models.

Lucas Krenn

Co- Founder

Lucas Krenn was a second-year financial mathematics student at McMaster was working as an ambassador for The Montreal Exchange. His areas of interest included equities, options, and futures trading, algorithmic trading techniques, research in order types, and HFT.

Graduated MIRC Members

Below are former MIRC alumni and their descriptions. The majority of all graduated MIRC members had found successful jobs in the financial sector or other related fields. If you wish to contact them please email us at mirc@ms.mcmaster.ca

Omar Hashem

Finance Chair

Omar Hashem was a Commerce student specializing in Finance and Economics. He was also head of the macroeconomic research at the McMaster Investment Council. His areas of interest included behavioral finance and fundamental research. Omar has now gone on to work at Bank of America - Merrill Lynch.

Sadisha Galappatti

Technical Chair

Sadisha Galappatti was a software engineering student with an interest drive for devops engineering and full stack development on mobile and web. His main interest for the application of software systems to finance. Sadisha has now graduated and gone on to work for the Royal Bank of Canada.
